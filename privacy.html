General Company Policy

The Ro team met while working at MIT on a data privacy project. Personal data and privacy is our foundation. It's something we think about all the time and continue to design into our products.

For us, one of the main motivations behind starting this project was to try and reverse some of the recent trends on data use by companies.

Privacy is not an afterthought for us. We're going to go out of our way to make sure your data is safe and belongs only to you. You have the final say on what gets done with it.
